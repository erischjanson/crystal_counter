Crystal Collector

Try to match the randomly generated number!

A random number will display on the screen.
There are four different crystals below. Each crystal is worth a certain number of points.
Every time you click on a crystal, your current score will increase by whatever number of points the crystal is worth.
The goal is to match your current score with the random number.
If you match the random number, you win! If you go over the random number, you lose. After each win or loss, your scoreboard will reflect your wins and losses and the game will refresh so that you can continue playing.
